Welcome to IYF Digital Learning Platform

Given that IYF is not allocated in all areas in Kenya, online learning will be a great alternative for those who cannot access the institution geographically. Also, IYF has high demand, thus unable to accommodate all interested individuals for the program. IYF online learning offers flexibility for students to learn at their own pace and schedule, making it easier for those with other commitments or those who cannot attend traditional school hours. It is also more cost-effective by reducing the need for physical infrastructure and transportation, making it a viable option for both the educational institutions and students.
